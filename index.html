SporTrips chains games you can actually drive between — set your dates, daily driving limit, and sports to get full routes.
Drive math: distances come from venue coordinates (great-circle distance × a 1.25 road-detour factor at ~100 km/h); a trip only links two games if you can cover the distance within your rest days at your daily driving limit. All distances are in kilometres.
Maps & data: interactive maps by Leaflet with CARTO / OpenStreetMap tiles. Schedules from ESPN’s public scoreboard API, matched to a built-in table of all 124 MLB/NBA/NHL/NFL venue coordinates. Start address geocoded by Photon / OpenStreetMap. If live data can’t be reached, SporTrips falls back to a season-aware sample schedule and tells you so.
